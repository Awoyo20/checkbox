THE OWO YOUTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY APPOINT SPOKESMAN The First General Meeting of The Owo Youth General Assembly was anchored by Folahanmi Odunayo via Zoom. The meeting surface like the first of it kind as there was massive turnout from Owo Youths both home and in diaspora. The meeting which was scheduled to hold for an Hour 7pm - 8pm, lasted for 2hours as Youths across globe cross fertilize ideas for the betterment of Owo Youths. The Meeting which centralized on Constitution Drafting Commitee, the Committee chaired by Arc. Oso Oluwaferanmi gave a comprehensive report regarding their achievement so far after a week of appointment. He promised that within the next two week the committee will present to the House. The Owo Youth General Assembly expressed concern on Leadership, this is to avoid mistake of the past. Therefore, it became imperative for members to be patient enough for the constitution to be ready before appointing or electing her Leaders. Be that as it may, for record and granting audience to the Press and Media, Desmond Awoyo was unanimously appointed as interim Spokeperson for The Owo Youth General Assembly. This is due to his political antecedents and his fearless efforts over time. The group urge him to discharge his responsibility within few weeks fearlessly. The meeting lasted with alot of programs lighted and to be achieve before the end of the year.
